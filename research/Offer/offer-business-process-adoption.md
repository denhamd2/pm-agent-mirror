## Feature Overview

**Feature Name:** Offer Business Process

## 📝 Description

The Offer business process enables you create an offer including compensation, generate and review documents such as offer letters, and renegotiate offers for candidates. This business process makes it easier to track and negotiate offer details for candidates.

---

## 📊 Adoption Metric

**Definition:** A business process using the Business Process Type of "Offer" ran at least once within the last 12 months.

**What This Means:**
- Tracks whether customers are using the Offer business process at all
- Binary metric: Either they used it (adopted) or they didn't
- Measured over a 12-month rolling window
