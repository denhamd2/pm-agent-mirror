## Executive Summary

This matrix tracks **Darwinbox**, **Keka HR**, and **Zoho Recruit** as the default **top three** India-regional competitors for Workday Recruiting. The market mixes **modern mid-market HR suites** and **value ATS** bundles with strong **SMS / WhatsApp** narratives and **AI screening** demos. **DPDP** and **local job boards** (e.g. **Naukri**) are **first-class** in **Q2 2026** India strategy; for **IN-E2E-003** align to `research/India/strategy-context-2026-03-28-IN-E2E-003.md` (**8 customer wins**, **DPDP** + **boards**, **AI matching** + **ATS parity**).

**28 March 2026 Pattern 1a scan (IN-E2E-003):** **Deployment Agent DA-IN003** classifies **native Workday Messaging (SMS) to Indian +91 numbers** as **True Gap** (no current support). **Triangulate** with **INDIA-E2E-001 / DA-IN001**, which framed **SMS to India** as **Workaround** via **third-party messaging** — **GTM:** native path **True Gap**; **candidate SMS outcome** may still be **Workaround** (**CPaaS / integration**) pending **PS + UAT**. **Multipost** to major India boards without a multiposter remains **Workaround** (**Broadbean-class** path). **Native WhatsApp** in core Recruiting UI and **semantic AI match without HiredScore / Workday AI SKUs** remain **True Gaps**. **Hindi** recruiter and candidate experiences are **Native**. **Enterprise** pressure: **Mar 2026** **SAP SmartRecruiters for SuccessFactors** integration news; **Oracle** **Recruiting Booster** + **agentic** India narrative. Keep claims **sourced**; route legal-sensitive copy through **060**.

**Regional emphasis for scans**: Native vs partner **+91 SMS** clarity, **DPDP**-aligned consent and retention, **local board** coverage via **Broadbean** validation, **AI matching** (HiredScore / Workday AI), high-volume hiring and **mobile** parity, **SAP / Oracle** AI + omnichannel benchmarks.

## Regional competitor profiles

### Darwinbox

- **HQ / scale:** India-founded **cloud HRMS**; **enterprise** recruiting module; **Forrester Wave** Strong Performer (vendor).  
- **Differentiators (web, Mar 2026):** **AI / ML resume screening** (NLP / skills claims, high-volume screening), **mobile-first**, **voicebot**, **responsible AI** messaging; **WhatsApp** / HR integrations (broader than recruiting — scope per deal).  
- **vs Workday:** **Omnichannel** and **single-vendor** HRMS story; **Workday** counters on **global** **scale**, **security**, **hire-to-pay**, **DA-IN003** **privacy Native** — **native +91 SMS** **True Gap**; **partner SMS** **Workaround** only when **validated**.

### Keka HR

- **Positioning:** **India** recruitment + ATS; **15+** channel **one-click** posting; **AI** JD and screening; **DPDP** compliance claimed on vendor pages; tight **payroll / HR** adjacency.  
- **vs Workday:** **Speed** and **local** **mid-market** packaging; **Workday** counters on **enterprise** **process**, **global** **template**, **Broadbean** **board** **governance**.

### Zoho Recruit

- **Positioning:** **ATS** with **Twilio SMS** and **Marketplace WhatsApp** extensions (in-product channel narrative); **multipost**; **Zoho** ecosystem bundle.  
- **vs Workday:** **TCO** and **omnichannel** **defaults**; **Workday** counters on **suite** **depth**, **DPDP** **programme** **fit**, **enterprise** **integrations** — **honest** **native** **channel** rows (**DA-IN003**) + **partner** paths where **validated**.

## Regional focus analysis

### Local compliance

**DPDP Act 2023** drives **notice**, **consent** (where required), **retention**, and **rights** expectations for **candidates**; **legitimate use** and **employment** processing need **060**-aligned interpretation. **Workday:** **Native** configurable consent, retention, purge (**DA-IN003**) — **not** a substitute for **customer** **legal** **design**.

### Local communication channels

**SMS** is **cultural default**; **DA-IN003** = **True Gap** for **native** Workday Messaging to **+91**; **partner / CPaaS** **Workaround** for **outbound SMS** (**triangulate** **DA-IN001**; **PS** validation). **WhatsApp** heavily used; **Workday** **True Gap** **native** **core** **UI** — **competitors** (**Zoho** Marketplace, suites) **market** **channels** **aggressively**.

### Local integrations

**Major India job boards:** **Naukri**, **Shine**, **Monster India** — **Workaround** via **Broadbean** or **connectors** (**DA-IN003**). **Validate** **Broadbean** **coverage** per **deal**; **010**: no **native** **board** **build** **recommendation**.

## Battle card quick reference (India)

• **Do say:** Unified **HCM + Recruiting**, **Hindi** support, **configurable** **privacy** **controls**, **bulk** / **mobile** **parity**, **hire-to-pay** when **Payroll** in scope, **Broadbean** **multipost** **strategy**, **HiredScore** / **Paradox** when **licensed**.  
• **Do not over-claim:** **Native** **Workday** **SMS** to **+91** (**DA-IN003** **True Gap**); **native** **WhatsApp**; **direct** **Naukri** **without** **multiposter**. **Partner** **SMS** only with **PS**-validated **integration**.  
• **Ask:** **Board** list, **SMS** **architecture** (native vs **CPaaS**), **WhatsApp** **requirements**, **AI** **governance** **needs**, **DPDP** **legal** **owner**, **SAP** / **Oracle** **incumbent** **AI** **story**.
