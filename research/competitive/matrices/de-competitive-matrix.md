## Executive Summary

This matrix tracks **Personio**, **Softgarden**, and **d.vinci** as the default **top three** Germany-regional competitors for Workday Recruiting. The market strongly favours **SMB and mid-market bundles** (HRIS + recruiting) and **agency-oriented** ATS (d.vinci). Co-determination and works council–related **hiring and change processes** appear frequently in evaluations; product claims must be **validated** (functional knowledge + Deployment Agent; **060** when legally sensitive).
