## Executive Summary

This matrix tracks **Cegid Digitalrecruiters**, **Beetween**, and **Flatchr** as the default **top three** France-regional competitors for Workday Recruiting. Buyers often expect **French-first** UX, local HR and hiring practice familiarity, and EU data protection alignment. Workday competes on **global enterprise** footprint, **unified HCM**, and depth of **recruiting + compliance** at scale; regional vendors compete on **local product fit**, **implementation ecosystem**, and **commercial packaging**.

**Regional emphasis for scans**: French language and localisation quality, EU/FR labour and hiring process narratives (validate carefully; **060** for legal-sensitive claims), local job board and partner landscape (**Broadbean** per **010**), mid-market vs enterprise win patterns.
