# France Competitive Matrix
**Last Updated**: 28 March 2026 | **Version**: v1.2 | **Maintainer**: Agent 101  
**Region**: France

## Executive Summary

This matrix tracks **Cegid Digitalrecruiters**, **Beetween**, and **Flatchr** as the default **top three** France-regional competitors for Workday Recruiting, plus **SAP SuccessFactors + SmartRecruiters** and **Oracle Fusion Recruiting** as **enterprise** benchmarks. Buyers often expect **French-first** UX, **multidiffusion** to local job boards, **manager-friendly** workflows, and **RGPD** alignment. Workday competes on **global enterprise** footprint, **unified HCM + Recruiting + Payroll** (including **France** statutory touchpoints such as **DPAE** when Payroll is in scope), **GDPR-class** privacy automation, and **suite** security; regional vendors compete on **local board reach**, **time-to-value**, and **packaged** **AI**/**automation** narratives.

**Regional emphasis for scans**: French language and localisation quality; **EU** / **FR** labour and hiring claims (**060** for legal-sensitive statements); local job boards via **Broadbean** per **010**; **EU AI Act** transparency for hiring **AI**; mid-market vs enterprise win patterns.

## Regional competitor profiles

### Cegid Digitalrecruiters

- **HQ / footprint:** Cegid; strong **France** GTM and FR product pages.  
- **Positioning:** **ATS + CRM** for **multi-site** employers; **AI** CV analysis and scoring; **HR marketing** (email, **SMS**, QR, ads).  
- **Claimed strengths (web, 28 Mar 2026 pass):** **3,500+** boards / partners **one-click**; **20+** pool filters; **interview scheduling** with calendar sync; **mobile** for managers; AI CV summarisation / scoring on **FR** product pages (**verify** in **RFPs**).  
- **vs Workday:** **Native** **multidiffusion** count vs Workday **Broadbean** **Workaround**; **suite** depth and **payroll** vs **point ATS**.

### Beetween

- **HQ / footprint:** **France**; FR + EN sites.  
- **Positioning:** **Customisable** ATS; **multidiffusion** (**180+** boards per **beetween.fr** feature pages); **career site**, **referrals**, sourcing; **SMS**, video, **AI chatbot**; **AI matching** on database; **Google for Jobs**-oriented posting structure.  
- **vs Workday:** **Flex** and **speed** for **PME**/**mid-market**; **enterprise** **security**, **global** **scale**, and **HCM** **adjacency** favour Workday when table stakes are met.

### Flatchr

- **HQ / footprint:** **Boulogne-Billancourt**; **HR Technologies France 2026** exhibitor.  
- **Positioning:** **Hiring-manager**/**field**-centric ATS; **160+** boards **one-click**; automation of **relances** and planning; **HR Technologies France 2026** exhibitor (**hrtechnologiesfrance.com**).  
- **vs Workday:** **Simplicity** for **distributed** **managers**; **Workday** **wins** on **global** **process**, **reporting**, and **suite** when **requirements** exceed **ATS-only**.

## Regional focus analysis

### Local compliance and privacy

- **EU GDPR** and **France** expectations apply to **consent**, **retention**, **candidate rights**, and **hiring AI** transparency (**EU AI Act** — **060** for classification and claims).  
- **Workday (DA-FR001):** **Native** framework for **consent**, **privacy display**, **retention/purge** — **not** a substitute for **customer** **legal** **design**.  
- **France hosting** narratives from **local** vendors: **validate** **factually** per **deal** (Workday **data** **region** **strategy** via **official** **sources** / **SC**).

### Local communication channels

- **Email** and **SMS** are **standard** **France** channels; **WhatsApp** **less** **central** than **GCC** but **rising** in **candidate** **experience** **comparisons** vs **Oracle** **Recruiting** **Booster** **messaging**.  
- **Workday:** **DA-FR002** (**FR-E2E-002**, 28 Mar **2026**) — **SMS** **Workaround** (third-party + integration). **DA-FR001** had **Native** **SMS**. **Mandatory** **PS** / **SMSC** / **entitlement** **triangulation** with **GCC** **DA** **threads** before **global** **decks** **freeze** **SMS** **claims**.

### Local integrations

- **Job boards:** **Broadbean** **France** **multidiffusion** for Workday; competitors cite **100–3,500+** **boards**.  
- **Statutory:** **DPAE** and **Payroll** **France** **(DA-FR001)** — **differentiate** vs **ATS-only** **vendors** when **Payroll** **in** **scope**.
