## Executive Summary

This matrix tracks the **top three UK-regional** recruiting / HR tech competitors for Workday Recruiting: **Bullhorn**, **Access People HR**, and **Ciphr**. The UK market mixes **agency-heavy** workflows (Bullhorn), **mid-market HR + recruiting bundles**, and **SMB** suites. Workday typically competes on **enterprise scale**, **global consistency**, and **platform depth**; regional vendors compete on **UK familiarity**, **price**, and **tight HRIS + ATS packaging**.

**Regional emphasis for scans**: GDPR / UK GDPR posture (validate claims), agency vs corporate hiring norms, job board coverage via **Broadbean** (per **010**), local customer references, integrations with UK payroll and benefits ecosystems.
