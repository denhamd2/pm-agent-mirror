| **WhatsApp-first candidate comms** | Regional ATS and CRM recruiting tools with **official WhatsApp Business** flows | **Native WhatsApp in core UI** (see `india-native-whatsapp-v91` prototype, INDIA-E2E-005) remains differentiator work; **DPDP** consent UX must be first-class. |
| **Two-way email / threading** | Gmail/Outlook-centric workflows; some ATS expose **full thread on candidate** | **Gap:** recruiters still **leave Workday** to read replies; **profile-anchored thread** closes swivel-chair for India high-volume pods. |

| **High volume** | **Bulk actions**, **knock-out agility** at applicant scale (Greenhouse / iCIMS narratives in gap data) | Strengthen **batch comms**, **exclusion transparency**, and **SLA visibility** on reqs and offers. |

Treat **profile two-way email** as **fast-follow** UX that **complements** WhatsApp prototype work—same **ProfilePageLayout** / communication dock pattern, **email channel** rail, **thread object** in recruiting core.
