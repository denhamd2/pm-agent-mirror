## Market Insights (India: DPDP, Aadhaar, Fraud Prevention)

• **DPDP:** Third-party **implementation** guides reference **Rules** notification **November 2025** and **phased** obligations into **2026–2027** (e.g. consent managers, substantive compliance windows). Use **official** **MeitY** / **Board** sources in customer legal packs; **CADP** tracker is a **planning** **aid** only.  
• **Aadhaar:** **UIDAI**-grade **verification** remains **vendor** and **process** territory for **Workday**; **marketplace** **patterns** on **Zoho** set **buyer** **expectations** for **in-ATS** **ID** **checks**.  
• **Fraud:** **2026** **press** on **AI** **job** **scams** and **deepfake** **interviews** raises **bar** for **trust** **artefacts** (**verified** **employer**, **secure** **document** **exchange**, **human** **oversight**). **EU** **AI** **Act** **and** **GDPR** **patterns** in **060** still apply where **multinational** **deployers** **process** **EU** **data** **subjects**.

**Sources (sample):** [CADP DPDP implementation tracker](https://cadp.in/resources/guides/dpdp-implementation-tracker/), [TCSA DPDP Rules roadmap](https://www.tcsa.in/resources/dpdp-rules-2025-implementation-roadmap), [News18 AI fake job offers](https://www.news18.com/explainers/how-fake-job-offers-are-using-ai-to-trick-candidates-into-trafficking-how-to-spot-red-flags-shil-ws-el-10005699.html), [SMEStreet InCruiter deepfake](https://smestreet.in/technology/incruiter-identifies-ai-deepfake-in-recruitment-process-11258759/)
