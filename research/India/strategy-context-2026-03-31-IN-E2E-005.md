## Strategic Priorities (Q2 2026)

**Priority 1: GCC market readiness (strategic)**  
• **Description**: GCC expansion is the top regional bet for Q2; product gaps (messaging, nationalisation, Arabic, boards) block sales.  
• **Target outcomes**: 10 GCC customer wins; zero product-related deal blockers in GCC pipeline.  
• **Regional relevance (India)**: **Indirect**. India is not GCC; competitive scan and customer research should still note **global parity** items (messaging, compliance patterns) that India enterprises also expect, without displacing India-specific DPDP and local board narrative.

**Priority 2: AI candidate matching (differentiation)**  
• **Description**: HiredScore activation and AI ranking, shortlisting, and predictive time-to-fill; bias detection and explainability aligned to EU AI Act.  
• **Target outcomes**: 5 beta tenants on AI matching by end Q2; ~20% time-to-fill reduction for beta customers.  
• **Regional relevance (India)**: **High**. India high-volume hiring and skills-led markets align with rediscovery, grading, and explainability; **DPDP** and consent for AI/automated processing remain tension points for PMF and legal triangulation.

**Priority 3: Core ATS parity (table stakes)**  
• **Description**: Bulk actions, mobile recruiter experience, background check integrations, reduced scheduling friction (Paradox activation).  
• **Target outcomes**: Parity with SAP/Oracle on key workflows; fewer “missing basic feature” objections.  
• **Regional relevance (India)**: **High**. Bulk actions, mobile, BGV integrations, and scheduling are standard enterprise evaluators in India; job board coverage via **Broadbean** (not native builds) per product context.

## Competitive Positioning

**Workday differentiation**  
1. **Suite depth**: HCM + Recruiting + Talent + Learning versus point ATS.  
2. **AI-powered**: HiredScore + Paradox embedded in workflows versus manual processes.  
3. **Compliance-first**: GDPR, EU AI Act, global privacy posture.  
4. **Enterprise scale**: Security model, global rollout patterns.

**Competitive vulnerabilities**  
• Job board coverage gaps in niche regions (partner expansion before native build).  
• Mobile recruiter experience versus strongest competitors.  
• Interview scheduling requires Paradox activation (not automatic win in bake-offs).

**Key messaging (sales and GTM)**  
• One TA suite with **thoughtful AI** (human-in-the-loop, explainability) rather than AI sprawl.  
• **Efficient, compliant hiring at global scale** with regional packs (India: DPDP, local boards, high-volume workflows).
