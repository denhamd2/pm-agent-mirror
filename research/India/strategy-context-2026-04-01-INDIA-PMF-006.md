## Strategic Priorities (Q2 2026) `[Q2 doc]`

**Executive summary (document)**: Q2 2026 focuses on **global expansion** (GCC, APAC) and **AI-powered efficiency** while maintaining competitive parity in core ATS capabilities `[Q2 doc]`

**Priority 1: GCC Market Readiness (Strategic)**  
- **Why**: GCC expansion is 2026 strategic priority; product gaps block sales `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Key initiatives**: Candidate communication (WhatsApp and SMS; GCC cultural fit; 98% open rates); nationalisation compliance (KSA Nitaqat, UAE Emiratisation, Kuwait labor law reporting); localisation (Arabic RTL, country-specific workflows); job boards (Bayt.com, GulfTalent via Broadbean) `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Success metrics**: 10 GCC customer wins by end Q2; zero product-related deal blockers in GCC pipeline `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Competitive context**: SAP SuccessFactors and Oracle have GCC-specific features; we're catching up `[Q2 doc]`

**Priority 2: AI Candidate Matching (Differentiation)**  
- **Why**: HiredScore acquisition needs to show ROI; AI is competitive differentiator `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Key initiatives**: Activate HiredScore for high-volume customers; AI-powered candidate ranking and shortlisting; predictive analytics for time-to-fill; bias detection and explainability (EU AI Act compliance) `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Success metrics**: 5 beta tenants using AI matching by end Q2; 20% reduction in time-to-fill for beta customers `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Competitive context**: Paradox (scheduling) and HiredScore (matching) give us AI edge over Oracle/SAP `[Q2 doc]`

**Priority 3: Core ATS Parity (Table Stakes)**  
- **Why**: Can't win if we lack basic bulk actions, mobile, or integrations `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Key initiatives**: Bulk candidate actions (reject, advance, email); mobile recruiter experience improvements; background check vendor integrations (HireRight, Checkr); interview scheduling friction reduction (Paradox activation) `[Q2 doc]`  
- **Success metrics**: Feature parity with SAP/Oracle on key workflows; zero "missing basic feature" objections in sales cycles `[Q2 doc]`
