## Strategic priorities (Q2 2026) and India relevance

### Priority 1: GCC market readiness (strategic)

- **Description:** Close GCC product gaps that block sales: candidate messaging (WhatsApp/SMS), nationalisation reporting, Arabic RTL, local workflows; job boards via Broadbean.  
- **Target outcomes:** 10 GCC customer wins by end Q2; zero product-related GCC deal blockers.  
- **India relevance:** **Indirect.** Validates Workday’s playbook for **regulatory and local fit** (reporting, localisation, messaging norms). For India, mirror the pattern: **local compliance (DPDP)** and **distribution (local job boards via Broadbean)** are the parallel levers, not GCC-specific features.

### Priority 2: AI candidate matching (differentiation)

- **Description:** HiredScore activation, AI ranking/shortlisting, predictive time-to-fill signals, bias/explainability aligned to EU AI Act expectations.  
- **Target outcomes:** 5 beta tenants on AI matching by end Q2; ~20% time-to-fill improvement for beta cohort (per quarterly doc).  
- **India relevance:** **Strong, especially for HVH.** High-volume hiring and shortlisting at scale map directly to **Priority 2**. India competitive scans should stress **AI-assisted throughput**, **fairness/disclosure** expectations, and **activation** (not “build net-new matching engine”). **Paradox** remains interview-scheduling differentiation where activated.

### Priority 3: Core ATS parity (table stakes)

- **Description:** Bulk candidate actions, mobile recruiter experience, background-check integrations, reduced scheduling friction (Paradox).  
- **Target outcomes:** Parity with SAP/Oracle on key workflows; fewer “missing basic feature” sales objections.  
- **India relevance:** **Strong, especially for KYC.** Know Your Candidate spans **data quality, verification, duplicates, background checks, consent and transparency**; these sit in **parity and trust** alongside DPDP. Bulk actions and mobile are **HVH enablers**.

## Mission lens: Know Your Candidate (KYC) and High Volume Hiring (HVH)

Use this section to align **@competitive-intel**, **105**, **@pmf-analyst**, and **130**.

**Know Your Candidate**

- **Strategic hook:** Compliance-first positioning (DPDP, consent, data minimisation, auditability) plus **trust** in candidate data (identity, duplicates, documents, background screening integrations).  
- **CI prioritisation:** Map regional ATS and point solutions on **verification, deduplication, document UX, BGV integrations, consent capture, and AI disclosure** for screening.  
- **105 tension flags:** Where customers demand **deeper identity/verification** than roadmap emphasises, or **DPDP** operational detail beyond sales talking points, flag **strategy–customer tension**.  
- **PESTEL/SWOT / RICE:** Legal and Social factors should explicitly feed **KYC**; weaknesses/opportunities around **parity vs local specialists**.  
- **130 (Product Strategy slides):** Surface **DPDP + KYC** as one narrative thread; tie to enterprise trust and global rollout patterns.

**High Volume Hiring**

- **Strategic hook:** **Priority 2 (AI matching)** + **Priority 3 (bulk/mobile/scheduling parity)**. HiredScore activation and throughput features are the primary product levers.  
- **CI prioritisation:** Competitors’ **high-volume workflows**, **bulk actions**, **recruiter efficiency**, **AI shortlisting**, and **scheduling** (including conversational tools).  
- **105 tension flags:** Recruiter pain at **scale** vs **beta/activation** limits or **mobile** gaps.  
- **PESTEL/SWOT / RICE:** Economic and Technological factors (labour market scale, digital adoption) support **HVH**; threats from **local ATSs** tuned for volume.  
- **130:** Connect **HVH** to AI differentiation and parity themes with India-specific proof points when evidence exists.
