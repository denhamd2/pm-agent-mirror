# Strategy Context — India Recruiting (INDIA-E2E-006)

**Date:** 7 May 2026  
**Mission:** INDIA-E2E-006  
**PM framing (conversation):** Customer interviews repeatedly surface **KYC / Know Your Candidate and fraud resistance**, **high-volume hiring** (BPO / frontline scale), **WhatsApp** as the preferred candidate channel, and **two-way email** (threaded replies, not blast-only templates) as operational reality alongside Workday.

## Market thesis

India remains a **scale-led** growth vector for Workday Recruiting: large hiring waves, agency and campus mixes, and **mobile-first** candidate behaviour. **DPDP 2023** raises the bar on **notice, consent, purpose limitation, and retention** for personal data used in recruiting—especially when using **non-email channels** (WhatsApp) and when **identity evidence** (government IDs, background checks) sits adjacent to **messaging** workflows.

## Strategic pillars (this pass)

1. **Trust and identity (KYC / fraud):** Employers expect **progressive identity assurance** without killing conversion; recruiters report **document fraud**, **duplicate identities**, and **OTP / eKYC friction** as recurring pain. Product narrative must pair **assurance** with **throughput** at India volumes.

2. **High-volume hiring:** Scheduling, knock-out, and **offer throughput** dominate; communications must support **templates + personalisation at scale**, **bulk guardrails**, and **auditability** when dozens to hundreds of offers move per day.

3. **Omnichannel candidate communications:** **WhatsApp** is table stakes for reach and read rates in many India segments; **email** remains the **system-of-record** channel for formal steps, attachments, and legal-sensitive content. **Two-way** behaviour (candidate replies threading back to the recruiter **on the candidate profile**) reduces swivel-chair between inbox and ATS and preserves **context for decisions**.

4. **Competitive and partner landscape:** Regional ATS and HCM players emphasise **native multipost**, **WhatsApp**, and **verification marketplaces**. Workday wins on **unified core** but must close **channel** and **verification orchestration** gaps called out in the India competitive matrix (see Step 4 scan).

## Implications for downstream PMF

The @pmf-analyst pass should **explicitly code** transcript and SME evidence against: `KYC-ASSURANCE`, `FRAUD-DUPLICATES`, `HIGH-VOLUME-THROUGHPUT`, `WHATSAPP-CONSENT`, `EMAIL-THREAD-CONTINUITY`, `OFFLINE-APPROVAL-SWIVEL`. The **E2E Handoff** table should include a **profile-anchored two-way email** recommendation as the **PM-selected prototype track** for Step 320 while retaining WhatsApp and KYC items as parallel roadmap bets.
