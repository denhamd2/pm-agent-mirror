## PESTEL Analysis — France (Recruiting / TA lens)

**Note:** Synthesised for **Workday Recruiting PMF** with **live** secondary sources; **060** should validate legal claims for customer-facing decks.

### Political

• EU **pay transparency** implementation deadline (**June 2026**) drives **job posting** content rules (salary bands, history questions) — affects **career site** and **req** templates in **France** and EU.

**Product implication:** Ensure **posting** and **application** flows support **EU** pay transparency fields and audit trails; align with **060** on wording.

**Product implication:** **Land-and-expand** and **suite** TCO stories matter for **mid-market**; **enterprise** deals need **parity** demos.
