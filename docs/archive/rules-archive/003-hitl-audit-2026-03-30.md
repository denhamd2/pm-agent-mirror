## Overlap Analysis

### 1. E2E Mode: Upfront Context vs Step 14 Strategic Intent

**Upfront Questions (Before Step 1):**
- Q1: "What's driving this PMF research?" → Categories: New market entry / Competitive threat / Customer demand / Strategic pivot / Compliance requirement / Refresh
- Q2: "Anything else I need to be aware of?" → Free text

**Step 14 PM Framing:**
- Agent presents drafts for Problem Statement, Success Criteria, Scope Boundaries
- PM provides: (4) **Strategic Intent (why now, why this)**, (5) Anything else to know

**OVERLAP DETECTED:** ⚠️

Both ask for strategic context, but at different levels:
- **Upfront Q1:** High-level category ("What type of driver?")
- **Step 14:** Specific narrative ("Why now, why this feature?")

**Severity:** LOW - Questions serve different purposes
- Upfront context informs the entire research phase (Steps 1-12)
- Step 14 narrative frames the specific recommendation for PRD writing

**Recommendation:** ACCEPTABLE - The upfront question is categorical, Step 14 requires narrative depth. The PM's upfront answer ("Strategic pivot") provides context for research, while Step 14 asks them to articulate the specific strategic narrative for the PRD.

**Possible refinement:** Change Step 14 to reference upfront context:
```
"You indicated this PMF research is driven by [upfront answer]. Now that we've selected [recommendation], please provide the Strategic Intent narrative: Why this feature? Why now? How does it align with [upfront driver]?"
```

This makes the connection explicit and reduces the feeling of repetition.

## Minor Refinement Implemented

### Connect Upfront Context to Step 14 in E2E Mode ✅ COMPLETE

**Finding:** In E2E mode, the upfront question asks "What's driving this PMF research?" and captures a category (e.g., "Strategic pivot"). Then at Step 14, we ask for "Strategic Intent (why now, why this)" without explicitly referencing the upfront answer.

**Impact:** User might not see the connection between their upfront answer and Step 14 request.

**Enhancement Implemented (AGENT-IMPROVE-007):**
Updated Step 17 in `001-e2e-pipeline-reference.md` to:
1. Retrieve PM Context from MISSION_LOG (upfront Driver + Additional context)
2. Include in AskQuestion prompt: "You indicated this research was driven by: [PM Context Driver]"
3. Reframe Strategic Intent question: "Why this feature now? How does it align with [upfront driver]?"

**Current Implementation (Step 17, line 334):**
```markdown
17. HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP (PM Framing): Retrieve PM Context from MISSION_LOG (upfront answers from before Step 1: Driver + Additional context). Orchestrator presents PM Kickoff Conversation with agent-proposed drafts for: (1) Problem Statement (from @pmf-analyst+105+@competitive-intel), (2) Success Criteria (2-3 metrics with research-based targets), (3) Scope Boundaries (what's NOT in v1). In the AskQuestion prompt, include: "You indicated this research was driven by: [PM Context Driver from MISSION_LOG]". Ask PM for: (4) Strategic Intent (why this feature now? how does it align with [upfront driver]?), (5) Anything else to know.
```

**Benefits:**
- Shows continuity between upfront context and PRD framing
- PM sees their earlier input being used
- Reduces feeling of "answering the same question twice"
- Maintains distinction (upfront = category, Step 14 = narrative)
