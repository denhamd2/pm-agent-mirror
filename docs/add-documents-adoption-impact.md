## Caveats
- `Median Time in Offer` is derived from monthly `Offer` BP completed-event duration medians in `dw.swh.bp_event_stats` (SANDBOX), not from an IUM metric ID.
- TTF applies `value > 0` filtering to avoid no-data zeros.
- Correlations are descriptive and per-customer; they do not imply causal impact.
- Rows with sparse month coverage (low pre/post month counts) should be interpreted cautiously.

- EA columns use the same pre/post windows and correlation definition as Offer. They are populated from Completed-event **median_days** in `data-employment-agreement-durations.ts` (PROD). For SANDBOX parity with the IUM and Offer columns, re-materialise tenant-month EA medians from SANDBOX and re-run `scripts/merge_ea_into_add_documents_adoption_md.py` after pointing it at that export.
- EA is only meaningful for tenants that run `Propose Compensation Offer/Employment Agreement`; empty EA cells indicate no Completed median coverage in the pre/post windows.
